The J.W. Sweetman
Overlooking O’Connell Bridge, the JW Sweetman has everything you could everything you’re looking for. This microbrewery hides a pub, café and restaurant, all in one traditional Irish setting. If you’re in or around the O’Connell street area, have a look inside, because guaranteed there will be something you like.
